TAX TYPES
One per line. The first entry is the default. Include VAT or EWT in the type name for automatic computation to work (e.g. "VAT", "EWT", "VAT+EWT").
EWT RATES (%)
One per line, as percentages. For example: 2 means 2%, 5 means 5%. These appear in the EWT rate dropdown on voucher lines.
⚠️ Changes take effect immediately after saving. Existing vouchers retain their saved tax type labels — only new voucher lines will use the updated list.

Loan Registry

Define loans and credit lines. Monthly principal, interest, and total payment are auto-computed.

Amortization Schedule

Three sections per loan: Interest only  ·  Principal payment  ·  Total payment. Click a loan in Section 1 to expand details.

Payment Method
